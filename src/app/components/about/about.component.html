Hello, nice to meet you! My name is Camila Herrera and I'm a venezuelan in love with languages. Foreigner languages have always been my pasion, I've learned over the years Japanese, German, Russian, Italian and English, and of course my mother language: Spanish. I decided to focus on English above all the others because it's my favorite language and I love everything about it. With that said, my objective in life is to help others become part of this beautiful multicultural community.

Professionally I've worked teaching English for 3 years now, I started teaching in my college's language courses following a structured plan and with students of all ages. Currently I'm a private teacher and I'm working with kids, teenagers and adults, adjusting always to the capacities and necessities of every student. I'm also teaching to people living abroad via programs like Skype and Zoom. I consider myself a very dinamic, cheerful and responsible teacher.

In the field of translation I have worked for 2 years now creating and translating captions for companies, as well as taking freelancer jobs to translate novels, documents and guides in general.

I'm a charismatic person and I'm always ready to give my best in order to help others accomplish their goals. I'm a woman who wants to grow professionally and personally doing what she loves the best... Languages.
